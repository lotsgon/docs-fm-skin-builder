## Getting Involved

### Share Your Skins

Created something cool? Share it with the community:

- Post on FM forums
- Share on Reddit (r/footballmanagergames)
- Upload to skin hosting sites

### Report Issues

Found a bug or have a feature request?

- Report in app via Help > Report Bug
- Include reproduction steps
- Attach relevant error messages

### Contribute to Documentation
